Great Callisto
Callisto is a moon of jupiter, and one of my personal favorite space objects. Maybe it's uniqueness, maybe it's its peculiar size, maybe it's its close resemblance to the city planet of Coruscant. All I know is, I'm a space nerd, and I think it looks cool. So if you happen to be a space lover, be sure to download this image and all of our others on this site! All downloads are free and in the highest quality available.
